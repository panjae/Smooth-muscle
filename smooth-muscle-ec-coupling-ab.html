üìã Clinical Application 13.1: Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS)

IBS is a GI disorder with intestinal cramping, flatulence, and altered bowel habits of unknown cause. Treatment includes antispasmodics like L-hyoscyamine (atropine analog) - a cholinergic receptor antagonist that blocks parasympathetic-induced smooth muscle contractility. Natural remedies include peppermint oil, which relaxes smooth muscle. However, it's contraindicated in GERD patients as it also relaxes the lower esophageal sphincter (LES), exacerbating symptoms.
